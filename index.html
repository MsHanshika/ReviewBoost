How to Use ReviewBoost

QR Code Generator

1. Enter your business name and Google Maps link

2. Click "Generate QR Code"

3. Download and use your custom QR code

4. When scanned, customers will provide contact info and be directed to your review page

Message Templates

1. Choose SMS or Email template type

2. Create your custom message

3. Set a delay time for when to send after purchase

4. Save your template for future use

Finding Your Google Maps Link

1. Search for your business on Google Maps

2. Click "Share" and copy the link

3. Paste the link into the QR code generator
